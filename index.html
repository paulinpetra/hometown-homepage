Visit the Aland Islands

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant archipelago in Scandinavia.

Top three activities to do in the Aland Islands

Look at old Windmills

Jan Karlsgarden is an open air museum where you can look at old windmills.

Go on a boat trip

The Åland Sea is a waterway in the southern Gulf of Bothnia,
between Åland and Sweden.

Smell the pine trees

Much like pine trees,
people in Åland have adapted to life on an island.
